I'm **Serdar Yavuz**, a Planning & International Operations Specialist with more than a decade of experience at **Medicert Uluslararası**, a certification body based in Izmir, Turkey. When the audits are done, I switch masks. I build web applications, automation tools, and digital systems using **Astro.js**, **JavaScript**, and **Python**. My Bosnian roots trace back to the Ottoman era, which is why this site speaks three languages.
